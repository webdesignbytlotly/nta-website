NTA MODELS SCOUTING INFORMATION PACK

This document is designed to explain, in simple language, how the NTA Models Scouting Division ("we", "our" or "the Scout") discovers and places new faces with modelling agencies. It is not a contract for signing - it is an information and terms document to help models and agencies understand how we operate and what to expect.

1. Quick Summary

We find new models (faces), prepare them for agencies, and introduce them to reputable local and international modelling agencies.

Our scouting service is free for models - there is no upfront fee. If a model signs with an agency because of our introduction and then completes paid work, we receive a 10% commission from the model's FIRST TWO (2) paid jobs resulting from that placement.

**Note on Submissions:** Unsolicited applications (those not scouted by an agent) incur a mandatory **$1 Admin Fee** upon submission (paid via the Get Scouted form) to cover administrative processing costs. If you are formally scouted, this fee is waived.

This document is for reading and understanding only - it does not require signing.

2. Our Role (What We Do and Don't Do)

What We Do:

  • Discover and evaluate new faces (models) through scouting.
  • Provide guidance on basic portfolio needs and how to present yourself.
  • Introduce models to agencies that we believe are a good fit, locally and internationally.
  • Follow up to make sure introductions are tracked and to help resolve simple communication issues.

What We Do NOT Do:

  • We are NOT the model's agent or manager once the model signs with an agency. The agency manages bookings, contracts and pay.
  • We do NOT negotiate job contracts on behalf of the model (that is the agency's role).
  • We do NOT guarantee work or success - placement depends on the agency and market demand.

3. How the Scouting Process Works (Step-by-Step)

  1. **Discovery:** We find potential models by social media, referrals, events or direct submissions. We look for potential and suitability for agencies (height, look, market fit, professionalism).
  2. **Initial Review:** We review submitted photos and basic details (age, location, measurements, experience). If a model looks promising, we advise on any simple fixes (lighting, clothing) and prepare a basic submission packet to send to agencies.
  3. **Introduction to Agencies:** We email or message the agency with the model's information and samples. We keep records of which agencies were contacted and when.
  4. **Follow Up & Placement:** If an agency expresses interest, they will usually request more material, a meeting, or invite the model to a casting. If the agency signs the model, the agency handles the contract and bookings.
  5. **Commission Tracking:** Once the model starts working, we track the first two paid jobs that came from the agency we introduced. We ask to be notified and to be paid the agreed commission.

4. For Models - What to Expect and Your Responsibilities

Eligibility & Materials:

  • Send clear, recent photographs: full body and headshots. Natural lighting and simple clothing are best.
  • Provide accurate personal information: full name, date of birth, height, measurements, contact details and any prior experience.
  • If you are under 18, you must have a parent or legal guardian's consent for us to present your materials.

How We Handle Your Materials:

By submitting photos and information, you give us permission to share those materials with agencies for the purpose of placement. We will not sell your images or information to unrelated third parties.

What You Must Do After Placement:

  • **Tell us immediately** if you sign with an agency we introduced. This helps us track commission for the agreed first two paid jobs.
  • If you receive money for a job that is part of the first two (after the agency signed you), pay the agreed 10% commission to us within **seven (7) days** of receiving your payment. If the agency pays us directly, we will receive the commission from them instead.

If You Change Agencies or Sign Independently:

  • If you sign with an agency that we did NOT introduce, we will not take commission.
  • If a model is uncertain about previous contacts, let us discuss it - open communication avoids misunderstandings.

5. Commission & Payment Structure (Clear USD Examples)

Commission Basics (Simple):

  • **Commission Rate:** 10% of the model's gross pay from the **FIRST TWO** paid jobs that result from placements with an agency we introduced.
  • **Currency:** Commission is calculated in United States Dollars (USD, \$). If the job pays in a different currency, we will convert to USD using the bank or payment processor exchange rate at the time of payment.

How Payment Can Flow (Two Common Ways):

  1. **Model Pays the Scout Directly:** The model receives the full payment from the job, then transfers the 10% commission to the Scout within 7 days.
  2. **Agency Deducts and Remits:** The agency withholds the Scout's 10% from the model's pay and pays us directly (agreed between agency & scout).

Examples (USD) Arithmetic Shown Clearly:

**Example A: Job 1 pays $1,000.00**
10% of $1,000.00 = $1,000.00 x 0.10 = **$100.00** (Commission).
The model keeps $1,000.00 - $100.00 = **$900.00**.

**Example B: Job 2 pays $500.00**
10% of $500.00 = $500.00 x 0.10 = **$50.00** (Commission).
The model keeps $500.00 - $50.00 = **$450.00**.

**Combined for first two jobs:**
Total commissions = $100.00 + $50.00 = **$150.00**.
Total kept by model = $900.00 + $450.00 = **$1,350.00**.

Important Payment Details:

  • **Payment Deadline:** Commission should be paid to the Scout within **seven (7) days** of the model receiving their job payment unless the agency agreed to pay on behalf of the model.
  • **Payment Methods:** We accept bank transfer (international bank details on request), PayPal, or other agreed payment processors. Please contact us at models@ntamodels.co.za to arrange the method.
  • **Invoicing:** We will issue an invoice for the commission when requested. Agencies can also provide written confirmation of deduction if they handle the payment.
  • If there is a dispute about whether a job is one of the first two resulting from our introduction, we will ask for documentation (booking emails, payment receipts) and try to resolve it collaboratively.

6. For Agencies - What to Expect and Your Responsibilities

Acknowledgement and Payment:

  • When we introduce talent, we ask agencies to acknowledge that the model was referred by NTA Models Scouting Division. Acknowledgement can be as simple as an email reply confirming interest.
  • Agencies may choose whether they deduct the 10% commission from the model and pay us directly, or allow the model to pay us. Either approach is acceptable, but we ask that payment reaches us within 7 days of the model receiving payment.

Vetting and Caution:

  • We expect agencies to vet talent as they normally would. We do not guarantee the model's success or fitness for every job.
  • Agencies must follow their own rules and local labour laws for casting, payment and working conditions.

Records and Confirmations:

Please provide simple confirmations (email or invoice) when a deduction is made or payment is sent to NTA Scouting. Clear records help avoid delays or disputes.

7. International Placements and Currency Conversions

Working with Agencies Outside South Africa:

We place models with agencies worldwide. Because international payments and currency conversions are involved, the final USD amount may vary slightly depending on bank or processor exchange rates and fees. We calculate the commission in USD. If an agency pays in a different currency, the exchange rate applied will be the rate at the time payment is received by the Scout.

Cross-Border Tax & Reporting:

Models and agencies should be aware of tax obligations in their own countries. We recommend consulting a tax professional. NTA Scouting will issue receipts/invoices showing commission amounts in USD to help with record-keeping.

8. Privacy, Images and Data Handling

How We Use Your Materials and Personal Data:

  • We use photos, videos and personal details only to introduce models to agencies and to promote them for placement opportunities. We do NOT sell your images or personal information to unrelated third parties.
  • We retain contact details and submission materials for a reasonable time to track introductions and commissions. If you want your information removed, email us at models@ntamodels.co.za and we will delete it on request unless we need to keep records for commission or dispute reasons.

Consent & Under-18s:

If you are under 18, a parent or legal guardian must consent to our presenting your materials. Please provide contact details for the guardian.

9. Legal Notes, Taxes, and Registration

Important Legal & Tax Information (Plain Language):

  • We are a scouting & placement service. We are not replacing the agency's legal responsibilities.
  • If you earn money from modelling or if you are paid a commission, you (models and agencies) should declare income to your local tax authority. For example, South African residents should declare income to SARS.
  • Operating without formal registration as a business is possible in many places, but registration (e.g., Pty Ltd in South Africa) gives legal protection and makes international work easier. We recommend those who earn regular income consult a local accountant or lawyer.

No Legal Advice Here:

This document does not replace legal advice. If you need legal or tax guidance, please speak to a qualified professional in your country.

10. Dispute Resolution and Contact Steps

How We Prefer to Solve Disagreements:

  • **Talk First:** If there is a payment or placement dispute, contact us at models@ntamodels.co.za with details and any receipts or emails.
  • **Evidence:** Provide booking emails, agency confirmation or proof of payment so we can check our records.
  • **Mediation:** If we cannot resolve the issue, we may suggest mediation or a neutral third-party to help.
  • **Legal Steps:** As a last resort, matters can be taken to legal channels - this is rare and we prefer to resolve amicably.

11. Frequently Asked Questions (Plain Language)

Q: Do models have to pay anything to be scouted?

**A:** No. Scouting and introductions are free. Commission only applies when a model is paid for work after being placed by an agency we introduced. Unsolicited applicants (non-scouted) are required to pay a **$1 Admin Fee** upon form submission to cover processing costs.

Q: Who enforces the commission?

**A:** We keep records of introductions and will ask for confirmation when a model is signed. Most agencies cooperate. If payment is not made, we will follow the dispute steps above.

Q: Will my photos be shared publicly?

**A:** We only share photos with agencies during the placement process. We do not publish or sell them without your consent. If you want them removed, contact us.

Q: How long does scouting take?

**A:** Times vary. Some models are contacted by agencies within days; others may take weeks or months depending on fit and demand.

Q: What if a model is offered work but it's not through the agency?

**A:** If work is not connected to the agency that signed the model through our introduction, the commission may or may not apply. We will review the situation case-by-case.

12. How to Proceed - Quick Checklist & Sample Messages

If You Are a Model:

  1. Prepare 2-4 clear photos (headshot + full body).
  2. Send your full name, DOB, height, measurements, location, and contact details to models@ntamodels.co.za OR submit via the **Get Scouted** page (which requires the $1 Admin Fee if not scouted).
  3. Tell us if you already have agency representation.

If You Are an Agency Receiving a Referral:

  1. Reply to the introduction email acknowledging receipt and interest (simple line is fine).
  2. If you accept the talent, include a note confirming whether you will deduct the 10% commission and remit it to NTA Scouting or instruct the model to pay directly.
