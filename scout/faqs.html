FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

If your question is not listed below, you are welcome to direct your query to our team via email at info@ntamodels.co.za.

Yes, the core scouting service is completely free! We are focused on finding and developing talent. If you were scouted directly by one of our agents and use their provided code, there is absolutely no charge to apply.

If you apply independently (without a scout code), a nominal $1 fee is charged. This is an administrative charge to process and securely store your application data. It helps us ensure that we are dedicating our time and resources to serious, committed candidates.

A Scout Code is a unique identifier provided to you by an official NTA Models scouting agent after they have approached you online or in person. This code exempts you from the $1 administration fee when submitting your application, confirming you are a direct discovery.

Your application is reviewed by our expert scouting team. If we see potential, we will contact you directly to discuss the next steps, which may include follow-up interviews, a portfolio shoot (if needed), and potential presentation to our network of associated agencies.

We aim to review all applications promptly. You should typically hear back from our team within 7 to 14 business days, especially if we are interested in moving forward. Please ensure your contact details are correct.

If we place you with an agency, you agree to pay NTA Models SA a **10% commission fee** from the first two successful bookings you receive with that agency. This is detailed in our Terms & Conditions and helps fund our continued talent scouting.

While our primary focus is in South Africa, we scout talent globally and work with associated agencies around the world. We look for models who are willing to travel and work internationally if the opportunity arises.

We are scouting for a diverse range of models, including commercial, fashion, plus-size, and specialized talent (e.g., fitness models, actors for commercials). We look for great potential, a professional attitude, and unique, striking looks.
