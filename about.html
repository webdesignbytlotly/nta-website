ABOUT US

Our Philosophy: Train, Scout, Place.

Established in 2020, NTA MODELS is a leading Model Academy and Scouting service dedicated to shaping the future of the industry. We provide comprehensive Academy Training covering runway technique, editorial posing, and industry standards, ensuring our talent is fully prepared for the global stage. Our commitment extends beyond training to active Scouting and Placement, where we discover new faces internationally and secure them signed contracts with prestigious agencies worldwide.

We pride ourselves on our holistic approach, focusing not just on external appearance but on building confidence, professional media skills, and personal branding for a sustainable, long-term career. We maintain transparent professional relationships with our models and clients, standing as a pillar of integrity in the fashion world.

NTA MODELS: Where potential meets professionalism.

To effectively manage the diverse needs of the modern market, NTA MODELS operates several specialized divisions, each focusing on a distinct area of the modeling and talent industry:

ACADEMY

Our academy division offers holistic curriculum that prepares aspiring talent artists for the real-world demands of the industry. Our programs are meticuosly designed to cultivate not just professional skills, but aslo the confidence, discipline strong necessary for long-term success.

SCOUTING & PLACEMENT

Scouting Division is the dedicated talent discovery arm of NTA Models and Talent Academy, a leading name in South Africa's fashion and entertainment industry. Our primary focus is to find and develop the next generation of top-tier models and talent. Our aim is to identify individuals with unique potential, raw charisma, and the ambition to succeed. We believe that true talent can be found anywhere, and we are committed to providing a platform for aspiring models from all backgrounds to launch their careers.
