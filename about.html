About Us

NTA Models was established in 2020 with a clear vision: to revolutionize the fashion and commercial modeling landscape in South Africa and beyond. We are a premier agency committed to prioritizing ethical management, transparency, and the holistic development of our talent.

We don't just manage careers; we build them. Our philosophy centers on cultivating confident, resilient, and well-rounded individuals prepared for the international stage. Our commitment extends to providing comprehensive support, ensuring a safe working environment, and facilitating long-term success for every model we represent.

Through continuous professional guidance and a focus on both local and global opportunities, NTA Models remains dedicated to being a transformative force in the industry.

The Academy

What We Do:

The NTA Academy provides essential, in-depth training for aspiring models. Our curriculum covers professional catwalk techniques, photography posing, personal grooming, and crucial industry etiquette necessary for building a successful portfolio and career foundation.

Core Values:

Scouting & Placements

What We Do:

This division is the launchpad for professional careers. We actively scout exceptional new talent and provide dedicated career management and strategic placements.
